What is Tandem?

The language learning app where millions of people teach each other.

How does Tandem work?

1. Join the community

Download the app and sign up for free. We review every application to make sure that the Tandem community remains safe and fun for everyone.

2. Find a partner

Once you’re in, Tandem makes it easy to find the right language exchange partner. Search by language, location, interests, and more.

3. Start talking!

Pick any way to talk: text, voice note, audio or video call. In-app correction and translation tools keep the conversation flowing.

Millions of language partners. Any language combination.
